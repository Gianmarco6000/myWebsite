About me

Hello everyone! My name is Gianmarco and I'm an 20 years old student of Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome. My passion started about 10 years ago when I began playing videogames with my first desktop pc. After playing some videogames, I started studying how they are made. In fact, my dream is to join the videogame industry as a programmer, and more precisely as a Computer graphic engineer. I just graduated from an Italian high school with a specialization in Computer Science and an overall score of 98 / 100 at the final exam. I started programming when I was 15 ,at school, and then it became a hobby, a intriguing and stimulating hobby that kept me full days in front of my pc aiming to solve new problems. One important thing that I learned throughout the years is that problem solving is one of the foundamental skill that a programmer must have to enhance his development workflow. I've studied various programming languages in different development environments so I am prepared to be a fullstack webdeveloper. In fact I've studied backend but also fronted with a touch of design thinking. In 2016, I studied videogames programming with C# and OpenGL at AIV - Italian Videogame Academy based in Rome, willing to follow my main dream. I'm also currently working as a freelance web developer because I think that one of the most important things of this job is to maintain contact with the client. My main goal is to produce a well structured code that can also be managed in the future. I am studying OpenGL and Machine learning during my spare time, to enhance my knowledge and maintain my skills up to date. I'm willing to learn and to improve my knowledge. I am a very self motivated, honest and reliable programmer that pays attention to details as well as deadlines.
